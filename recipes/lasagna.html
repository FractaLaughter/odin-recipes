Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans of Pasta Sauce
  • 2 Bricks of Frozen Spinach
  • Half Block of Cheese - Shredded
  • 4 Carrots
  • 2 Zucchini
  • 10 Button Mushrooms
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1 Full Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Box of Oven-Ready Lasagna Pasta
  • 500 ml Cottage Cheese

Recipe

The Sauce

  1. In large pot, on medium heat, begin frying cut up onions
  2. Add minced garlic and fry untli soft
  3. Add both cans of pasta sauce
  4. Add bricks of frozen spinach
  5. Cut carrots julian-style and add to the pot
  6. Slice zucchini and mushrooms and add to the pot

The Lasagna

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. In lasagna dish, place a small amount of sauce
  3. add 1 layer of lasagna pasta
  4. Spread a thin layer of The Sauce
  5. Spread layer of shredded cheese
  6. Add 2nd layer of pasta
  7. Spread full container of cottage cheese
  8. Add 3rd layer of pasta
  9. Add the remaining amount of The Sauce
  10. Spread remaining shredded cheese
  11. Cover with tinfoil and bake for 40 minutes with tray on lower rack
  12. Remove tinfoil and back for 5 minutes only
  13. Let stand for a minimum of 15 minutes before cutting
